## Hardware Pin Map (quick reference)

- **Clocks**:8MHz HXTAL

### ADC
- PA1: ADC0/1/2_IN1(ADC_POW)
- PA6: ADC0/1_IN6
- PA7: ADC0/1_IN7

### USART
- PA2: USART1_TX
- PA3: USART1_RX
- PB10: USART2_TX
- PB11: USART2_RX

### I2C
- PB6: I2C0_SCL
- PB7: I2C0_SDA

### PWM (TIMER0 complementary)
- PA8: TIMER0_CH0
- PA9: TIMER0_CH1
- PA10: TIMER0_CH2
- PB13: TIMER0_CH0_N
- PB14: TIMER0_CH1_N
- PB15: TIMER0_CH2_N

### LEDs (GPIO out)
- PB3: LED1
- PB4: LED2
- PB5: LED3
